### INOVEX – Agency Management Web Application  
**INOVEX** is a powerful and scalable web application designed to simplify agency operations. It enables efficient management of services, clients, quotations, orders, sales, accounts, and workflows, ensuring agencies can streamline their processes and scale with ease.  
Built with cutting-edge technologies, INOVEX delivers an intuitive user experience with clean, maintainable code.

#### 🎯 Key Features  
- **📊 Client & Service Management** – Organize and manage clients, services, and contracts seamlessly.  
- **💸 Quotation & Order Tracking** – Create, send, and track quotations and orders with real-time updates.  
- **📈 Sales Monitoring** – Get detailed insights into sales performance through automated reports and analytics.  
- **🛠️ Workflow Automation** – Automate workflows, tasks, and project management to improve efficiency.  
- **🔒 Secure Authentication** – Implement JWT-based secure authentication and role-based access control.  
- **📱 Fully Responsive** – Mobile-first design, ensuring optimal performance across devices.
