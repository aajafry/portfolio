### Ecomart – Multi-vendor eCommerce Platform  

**Ecomart** is a scalable and responsive **multi-vendor eCommerce application** developed using the **MERN stack**. It streamlines product management, order processing, and vendor collaboration, making it ideal for organizations and individual businesses.  

The platform features advanced management of **categories, products, coupons, orders, shops, and user accounts**, providing seamless solutions for both vendors and customers.

#### 🎯 Key Features  
- **🛒 Multi-vendor Support** – Enables multiple vendors to manage and sell products independently.  
- **🔐 JWT Authentication** – Secure user access with role-based authorization.  
- **🏪 Shop Creation & Management** – Vendors can establish and manage digital storefronts.  
- **🎟️ Coupon & Discount System** – Fully functional discount and promotional code management.  
- **📸 Image Uploads with Cloudinary** – Seamless media uploads and storage via Cloudinary.  
- **📱 Mobile-Responsive UI** – Designed with **Tailwind CSS** and **Shadcn-UI** for optimal performance on all devices.  
- **📦 Order Tracking & Inventory** – Manage stock levels, track orders, and oversee logistics.  
- **🛠️ Admin Dashboard** – Centralized control for overseeing vendors, products, and transactions.
