### logiEx – Multi-Inventory Management System  
**logiEx** is a responsive and scalable multi-inventory management application designed to streamline operations for businesses handling inventory, shipments, and logistics.   
Built on the **PERN stack** (PostgreSQL, Express.js, React, Node.js), logiEx offers features like user and role management, employee and vendor tracking, and detailed monitoring of purchases, sales, transfers, and vehicle logistics.  
With a clean, modern interface powered by **React**, **Tailwind CSS**, and **Shadcn-UI**, and robust backend functionality secured by **JWT authentication**, logiEx simplifies the complexities of inventory management for organizations of any size.

#### 🎯 Key Features  
- **📦 Inventory Management** – Track and manage products and supplies updates.  
- **🚚 Shipment & Transfer** – Seamlessly handle product transfers and shipment logistics.  
- **🧑‍💼 User & Role Management** – Implement role-based access for users, employees, and vendors.  
- **📊 Sales & Purchase Tracking** – Record and monitor sales, purchases, and returns.  
- **🚗 Vehicle Management** – Assign and track vehicles for delivery and product movement.  
- **🔒 Secure Authentication** – Role-based authorization and JWT-secured endpoints.  
- **📱 Fully Responsive** – Optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile platforms.
