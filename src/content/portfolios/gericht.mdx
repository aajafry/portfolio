### Gericht – Restaurant Website Template  
**Gericht** is a **fully responsive restaurant website template** crafted with **React and CSS**.  
This template focuses on **modern UI/UX** practices, ensuring a **smooth, immersive experience** across all devices.  

Whether for a restaurant business or food delivery platform, **Gericht** provides an elegant, customizable solution to showcase menus, and promote services.
