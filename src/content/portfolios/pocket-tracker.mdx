### Pocket Tracker – Income and Expense Management  
**Pocket Tracker** is a sleek, intuitive income and expense tracking application developed using **Vue 3** and the **Composition API**.  
It enables users to manage their finances with ease, featuring **secure authentication (JWT)**, **dark/light mode** toggling, and a fully responsive design.  
Built with **atomic design principles**, Pocket Tracker emphasizes **modular, reusable components** for scalability and maintainability.
